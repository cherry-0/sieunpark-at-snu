About Me

I am a PhD candidate in the Interdisciplinary Program in Artificial Intelligence at Seoul National University (SNU), advised by Prof. Youngki Lee in the Human-Centered Computer Systems Lab.

I am interested in how large language models and mixed reality interfaces can be applied to build usable, personalized, and socially grounded systems. My work is driven by a commitment to designing technologies that are transparent, supportive, and aligned with human needs in everyday settings.

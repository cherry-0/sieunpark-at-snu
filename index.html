About Me

Hi! I am a first-year PhD candidate in the Interdisciplinary Program in Artificial Intelligence at Seoul National University (SNU), advised by Prof. Youngki Lee in the Human-Centered Computer Systems Lab.

My research focuses on building human-aligned AI systems that are usable, adaptive, and grounded in real-world contexts. I explore how large language models and mixed reality interfaces can support people in everyday tasksâ€”especially in areas where interaction is socially sensitive, privacy is critical, or guidance is needed in situ.

Currently, I work across three application domains:

  • Privacy-aware collaboration in virtual environments
  • Adaptive assistance for learning and physical interaction
  • LLM-powered task support on mobile platforms

My goal is to design systems that not only understand user intent and context, but also respond in ways that enhance learning, agency, and trust.

I am always open to conversations about research or related ideas. Feel free to reach out at: sieunpark [at] hcs [dot] snu [dot] ac [dot] kr.

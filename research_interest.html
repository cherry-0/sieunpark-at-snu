My ongoing work spans three application domains:

Privacy-Aware Collaboration in Virtual Environments

– PRISM, an intelligent object-sharing system for collaborative VR, dynamically adjusts shared object visibility based on user roles, proximity, and interaction context to balance privacy and usability.

In-Situ Adaptive Assistance

– TaleTrain, a real-time scaffolding system that interprets children’s utterances during storytelling and guides caregivers with graduated prompts

– CRAFT, a mixed reality system that provides situated guidance for interacting with complex physical objects based on the user’s environment and object state.

LLM-Powered Task Assistance

– Lumo, a system that enables language-based interaction with mobile apps. It leverages LLMs and in-context demonstrations to incrementally improve inference quality and personalize the experience to the user’s intent.

Across these efforts, I aim to create human-aligned AI systems that enhance learning, agency, and trust in everyday technologies.

I’m always happy to connect with others interested in similar visions!
